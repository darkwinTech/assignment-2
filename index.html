Predicting Air Quality Levels Based on Pollution Indicators

This project applies machine learning (KNN, SVM, and Deep Neural Networks) to classify air quality levels (Good, Moderate, Poor, Hazardous) based on pollution indicators such as PM2.5, PM10, NO₂, SO₂, CO, temperature, humidity, industrial proximity, and population density. The study evaluates models for accuracy, precision, and recall, concluding that SVM provides the most balanced performance for real-world applications.
